NUTELLA AND PEANUT BUTTER SANDWICH

INGREDIENTS

  • NUTELLA
  • PEANUT BUTTER
  • HALF BANANA
  • BUTTER,softened
  • SANDWICH BREAD

METHOD

  1. Heat a skillet to medium heat.
  2. Spread one side of the bread with peanutbutter and the other side with nutella
  3. Add slices of banana and close the sandwich
  4. Butter one side of the bread and place on the heated skillet
  5. Spread a layer of butter on the other side while the sandwich is cooking
  6. Cook until lightly golden and flip to the other side
  7. Cook again until lightly golden and remove from the heat
  8. Let cool for a moment so as to not burn your mouth, and then enjoy
